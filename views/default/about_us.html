About Us

Our service allows you to select dishes to create a menu so that we can bring home cooked meals to your door.
Use this handy application to place your orders and set up and/or edit a schedule so that we can prepare for your order in advanced.


How to use the application

Use the options above to make/view a menu or schedule. Or you can click "Home" to view your calendar and information on your next meal.
